About Dr MO

Dr Moataz Mohamed Gomaa Abdelrahman (PhD, University of Miskolc) is a computational geophysicist and software developer specializing in inversion, physics-informed neural networks (PINNs), and data-driven subsurface modeling. He builds scientific software solutions for energy and mining industries using Python and MATLAB, bridging academic innovation and industrial application.

Services

Scientific Software Development

Custom tools in Python and MATLAB for inversion, signal processing, and AI-based interpretation.

Physics-informed neural networks for petrophysical intelligence and subsurface simulation.

Research Highlights

Dr MO leads projects combining physics-based modeling and AI to improve subsurface characterization and decision support. Focus areas include PINN inversion, hybrid learning systems, and AI-accelerated geophysical workflows.
